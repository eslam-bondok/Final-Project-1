What is trading and how does it work?

Financial trading is no different to any other form of trading: it’s about buying and selling assets with the aim of making a profit. Discover key concepts, participants and markets involved in financial trading. Trading is the buying and selling of financial instruments in order to make a profit. These instruments range from a variety of assets that are assigned a financial value that can go up or down – and you can trade on the direction they’ll take.
